# Library & Further Action

Dive deeper, meet people doing the work, and plug into projects that make the freed market real.

## Starter Reading

- **Benjamin Tucker —** *Instead of a Book*: essays on monopoly busting and mutual banking.
- **Kevin Carson —** *Studies in Mutualist Political Economy* and *The Homebrew Industrial Revolution* for decentralized production.
- **Noam Chomsky —** interviews on anarcho-syndicalism and voluntary federation.
- **Elinor Ostrom —** *Governing the Commons* for real-world examples of shared resource stewardship without central control.
- **David Graeber —** *Debt: The First 5000 Years* for how obligation and credit shaped societies before modern states.

## Places to Learn by Doing

- **Platform co-op directories** like [platform.coop](https://platform.coop/) to find or start cooperative digital services.
- **Mesh networking communities** such as NYC Mesh, Guifi.net, and neighborhood projects in your area.
- **Credit unions and CDFIs** that reinvest locally instead of extracting fees.
- **Makerspaces and fab labs** where you can learn repair, fabrication, and open hardware design.
- **Mutual aid networks** that prove community safety nets work when bureaucracy fails.

## Storybank: Wins to Share

- Community solar co-ops reducing bills and keeping profits local.
- Cities that legalized street vending, boosting household incomes and food diversity.
- Right-to-repair victories forcing manufacturers to unlock manuals and parts.
